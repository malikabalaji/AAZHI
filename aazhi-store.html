Our Story

Born from the Waves of Tamil Nadu

AAZHI — meaning ocean in Tamil — celebrates the ancient textile heritage of the Coromandel Coast. Our fabrics carry the spirit of the sea — fluid, powerful, and endlessly beautiful.

How to measure

  • Bust / Chest — measure around the fullest part, keeping the tape level and parallel to the floor.
  • Waist — measure the narrowest part of your torso, usually just above the navel.
  • Hip — measure the widest part of the hips, about 8" below the waist.
  • Shoulder — measure across the back, seam to seam.
  • Inseam — from the top of the inner thigh straight down to the ankle bone.
  • Use a soft tape, measure over light clothing, and don't pull tight — leave a finger of slack.
